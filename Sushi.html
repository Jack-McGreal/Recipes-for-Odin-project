Delicious Sushi

description: this is sushi and its made of fish and rice... originates from Nipon (Japan)

Ingredients:

  • -rice
  • -fish (but special type, i.e. no parasites)
  • -seaweed

steps:

  1. cut fish
  2. place seaweed down
  3. spread rice over the seaweed evenly
  4. lay the finely cut fish on the rice
  5. roll up the seaweed with the contents included
  6. bam, you have some sushi
